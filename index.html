Privacy Policy

App Name: Shopping Calculator +

Effective Date: February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Shopping Calculator + (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Shopping Calculator + only stores information that users manually enter into the app, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Shopping lists created by the user
  • Items added to shopping lists (names, prices, quantities, totals)

We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking data.

How We Use Information

The information is used only to provide core app features, including:

  • Saving and managing your shopping lists
  • Sharing lists with other users (only when you choose to share)
  • Syncing shared list updates

Data Storage

User data is stored on a dedicated server and database. This is required to support syncing and list-sharing features.

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Your data is only shared with other users when you choose to share a shopping list with them.

User Control & Deletion

  • You can edit or delete your shopping lists at any time within the app
  • You may request account or data deletion by contacting us

Children’s Privacy

Shopping Calculator + does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy
